[id: faq_001]
category: financing
question: Do you offer financing or lease options?
answer: Yes. We offer both purchase financing and lease programs. Financing terms range from 24 to 84 months with rates starting at 2.9% APR for qualified buyers. Lease terms are available at 24, 36, and 48 months with mileage options of 10,000, 12,000, or 15,000 miles per year. Applications can be started online or in-dealership.
tags: financing, lease, purchase, APR

[id: faq_002]
category: financing
question: What credit score is needed to qualify for financing?
answer: We work with a range of lending partners. A score of 680 or above typically qualifies for our best rates. Scores below 680 may still qualify through our secondary financing partners, though terms will differ. Pre-approval is available online and does not affect your credit score.
tags: financing, credit, pre-approval

category: financing
question: Can I trade in my current vehicle?
answer: Yes. We accept trade-ins for all makes and models. An appraisal can be completed online using our trade-in tool or in person at the dealership. The appraised value is applied directly toward your purchase or lease.

question: How do I schedule a test drive?
answer: Test drives can be scheduled online through the booking tool, by calling the dealership directly, or through the virtual showroom assistant. Select your preferred model, trim, and time slot. [id: faq_009]
category: warranty
question: What warranty comes with a new vehicle?
answer: All new Aether vehicles include a 5-year / 60,000-mile basic vehicle warranty, an 8-year / 100,000-mile battery and drivetrain warranty, and a 7-year corrosion perforation warranty. Roadside assistance is included for 5 years.
tags: warranty, battery, drivetrain, coverage

[id: faq_010]
category: warranty
question: Are software and over-the-air updates covered under warranty?
answer: Yes. Software updates are provided at no charge throughout the vehicle's lifetime, including vehicles out of the standard warranty period. Hardware failures caused by a software update are covered under warranty regardless of mileage.
tags: warranty, software, OTA-updates

---
[id: faq_011]
category: warranty
question: What is not covered by the warranty?
answer: Normal wear items such as brake pads, tires, and wiper blades are excluded. Damage caused by accidents, misuse, unauthorized modifications, or failure to follow maintenance guidelines is also excluded. Cosmetic damage such as paint chips and interior wear are not covered after 30 days from delivery.
tags: warranty, exclusions

[id: faq_012]
category: charging
question: What home charging equipment do you provide or recommend?
answer: We recommend installing a Level 2 home charger (240V, 48A circuit) for overnight charging. We partner with ChargePoint and Grizzl-E for hardware. Installation coordination is available through the dealership. The Standard and Long Range models fully charge overnight in 8 to 11 hours on a Level 2 charger.
tags: charging, home-charging, Level-2, installation

[id: faq_013]
category: charging
question: What public charging networks are compatible?
answer: All Aether vehicles support CCS2 (Combined Charging System) and are compatible with all major public networks including Electrify America, EVgo, Blink, and ChargePoint. The in-vehicle navigation automatically routes to compatible stations based on battery level.
tags: charging, public-network, CCS2, navigation

question: Where can the vehicle be serviced?
answer: Service is available at all authorized Aether service centers. Mobile service appointments are also available for software updates, minor repairs, and diagnostics — a technician comes to your location. Major repairs and body work require an in-center visit.

[id: faq_019]
category: service
question: How often does the vehicle need scheduled maintenance?
answer: Aether electric vehicles have no oil changes, transmission fluid, or spark plug replacements. Recommended service intervals are every 12 months or 12,000 miles, covering brake inspection, tire rotation, cabin air filter, and a full systems diagnostic.
tags: service, maintenance, schedule, electric

question: Can I sell the vehicle back to the dealership?
answer: Yes. We offer a guaranteed buyback program for vehicles within 3 years of original purchase date that meet mileage and condition requirements. Vehicles outside this window are accepted as trade-ins at market value. Contact a sales advisor for a buyback quote.
